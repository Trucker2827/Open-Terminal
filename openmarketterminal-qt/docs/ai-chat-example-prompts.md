# AI Chat — 100 Example Prompts

A curated list of natural-language prompts you can paste into the in-app AI Chat. The assistant fulfils each one by calling the terminal's own tools — SEC EDGAR filings, market quotes, news, crypto, macro and government data, the M&A/valuation playbooks, reports and notebooks. Everything here works on the app's free/built-in data; nothing requires a paid connector. Slash commands like `/comps`, `/dcf`, `/lbo`, `/3statement`, and `/earnings` each take a ticker (e.g. `/dcf AAPL`).
