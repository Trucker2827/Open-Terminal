# v0.3.18 — News: video-aware READ FULL

Many news items (most Bloomberg entries, YouTube/Vimeo links) are video pages
with no article text — the short intro is all there is. READ FULL now
recognises video URLs and says so ("📹 Video item — the text above is the full
available text; open in your browser to watch the clip") instead of trying to
fetch a body that doesn't exist and showing a misleading "hard paywall" note.
