# v0.3.9 — Example prompts you can click in AI Chat

The AI Chat welcome card now surfaces real example prompts as **clickable
chips** — tap one to run it. It's the in-app front door to the 100-prompt
library added in v0.3.8.

## New

- **Clickable example-prompt chips.** When you open a new chat, the welcome card
  shows a handful of concrete, runnable prompts (e.g. "Build a DCF for MSFT with
  a sensitivity table", "Top institutional holders of AAPL", "What's BTC trading
  at and the 24-hour change?") drawn from across the terminal's capabilities.
  Click a chip and the assistant runs it.
- **"More examples ↻"** reshuffles the chips to a different random set, so you
  can browse a variety without leaving the chat. The full library of 100
  categorized prompts lives in `docs/ai-chat-example-prompts.md`.

## Notes

- Chips span quotes, SEC financials, valuation (comps/DCF/LBO), ownership, news,
  crypto, macro, government data, M&A, portfolio, technicals, reports, notebooks
  and multi-step workflows — everything runs on the app's own free/built-in data.
