# v0.3.19 — News: transcribe video items

Video news items have no article text — but most have captions. A new
**TRANSCRIBE** button (shown on video items) pulls the video's caption track,
cleans it into a readable transcript, and shows it in the panel. The transcript
is shared with **ANALYZE**, so you can run your local AI model on what the video
actually says.

Works for YouTube/Vimeo and any source whose captions are accessible. Publishers
that block automated access (e.g. Bloomberg, HTTP 403) show an honest note.
Speech-to-text for caption-less videos (Whisper) is a possible future addition.
