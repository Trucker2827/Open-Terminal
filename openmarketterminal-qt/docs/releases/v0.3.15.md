# v0.3.15 — OECD: Long-term Interest Rate added (6 datasets)

Adds **Long-term Interest %** to the OECD panel — actual long-term government
bond yields (~10-year) from OECD's Financial Market dataflow, monthly, verified
across US/Germany/Japan (US 10y ≈ 4.5%). It's distinct from "Short-term
Interest %" (Economic Outlook).

OECD now has 6 working datasets: Real GDP Growth %, CPI index, Unemployment
Rate %, Short-term Interest %, Long-term Interest %, Current Account (USD).
