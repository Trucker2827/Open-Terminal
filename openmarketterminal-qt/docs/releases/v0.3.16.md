# v0.3.16 — News: full-article reading + local-AI analyze

The News panel's article detail now does two powerful things:

- **READ FULL** — fetches the complete article from the publisher (bundled
  headless browser) and shows the whole story instead of the short feed
  snippet. Works on open and soft-paywalled sources; genuinely hard paywalls
  (server-gated behind login/payment) can't be bypassed and show an honest note.
- **ANALYZE** — now runs your **active chat model (e.g. Ollama)** on the *full*
  article, not a cloud service. The analysis appears inline in the News panel
  **and** opens a new AI Chat session (article + analysis) so you can ask
  follow-up questions with full context.
