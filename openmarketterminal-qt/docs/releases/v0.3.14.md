# v0.3.14 — OECD economics data works

OECD redesigned their SDMX API, so every OECD dataset was returning an error
(internally HTTP 422; the panel showed a misleading frequency/key error). The
OECD fetcher has been rebuilt against the current API: full dimension keys,
3-letter ISO country codes, current dataflow IDs, and in-response series
selection.

Working datasets (verified across US, Germany, Japan, Spain, UK):
- **Real GDP Growth %** — OECD Economic Outlook
- **CPI (index, all items)**
- **Unemployment Rate %**
- **Short-term Interest %** — OECD Economic Outlook
- **Current Account (USD)** — OECD Economic Outlook

Notes:
- "GDP Forecast" was removed — OECD's real-GDP series already covers the
  projection years, so it duplicated "Real GDP Growth %".
- Datasets are labelled by what they are (growth/index/rate), not as levels,
  because OECD's absolute-level series are too sparsely populated to rely on.
- The frequency selector applies to CPI; the Economic Outlook series are annual.
