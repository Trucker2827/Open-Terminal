# v0.3.17 — News: stronger full-article fetch

READ FULL and ANALYZE now try much harder to get the complete article, using
the same techniques as reader browser-extensions:
- a cookieless load with a **search-crawler user-agent** + Google referer
  (publishers serve full text to crawlers; cookieless defeats free-article meters),
- **stripping paywall/overlay/modal elements** and restoring scroll/blur,
- an **archive.today fallback** when the direct fetch comes up short,
- keeping the longest result.

This reliably pulls full text on open and soft-paywalled sources and on articles
that have an archive snapshot. Genuinely hard paywalls with aggressive
bot-detection (e.g. Bloomberg) can still block a headless fetch, and video pages
have no article text to extract.
